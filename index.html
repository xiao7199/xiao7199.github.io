Xiao Zhang

I'm a final-year CS Ph.D student at the University of Chicago, where I primarily work with Michael Maire on computer vision. During my Ph.D., I focused on unsupervised representation learning using generative models. My research aims to develop scalable algorithms for representation learning applicable to real-world scenarios and complex image/video generation.

Before starting my Ph.D., I worked as a graduate research assistant with Jianbo Shi.
